TFT Damage Calculator

In the game Teamfight Tactics, the amount of damage you deal/take depends on the units left on the field, and also the stage you are on.

You can find what stage you are on by looking at the top of the screen while in a game of TFT. It is the first number on the left.

Please enter valid numbers, otherwise you will receive an error.
